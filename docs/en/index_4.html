Why we need self protection

1.If we were sick, the doctors will try their best to save us;
2.If all of us got sick, due to short of hands, the doctors can't help all of us.
3. If doctors got sick or exausted, there would be no one can save us.

Key points for self protections

1.Conscious home isolation

The rate of human-to-human transmission of NCP is very high, reducing the frequency of close contact with others initatively can greatly reduce the possibility of self infection.

2. Wearing medical maskers when going outside

Each one could be the infectious source no matter was symptomatic or asymptomatic, wearing medical maskers can not only protect yourself but greatly reduce the chance of transmission

3. Avoid to touch the public goods

You will never notice if the laster person who touched the public goods is a virus carrier or not. Please try to minimize unnecessary contact with public goods, E.g. elevator buttons, store door handles, communal dining tables and chairs.

4.Pay attetion to the hands hygiene

Pls wash your hands thoroughly according to the seven-step washing technique after you back from public place

5. Good housekeeping

Want to maintain the sanitation of living environment, carry out precautionary disinfection appropriately, but the safety that should pay attention to disinfectant is used.
